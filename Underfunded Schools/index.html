In the United States there a many schools that are underfunded, which prevent kids from having a strong foundation for higher education. Public school funding in the United States comes from federal, state, and local sources, but because nearly half of those funds come from local property taxes, the system generates large funding differences between wealthy and impoverished communities.

1. How you can help? You can donate to help the schools that are underfunded to create better education programs and more classroom supplies where each student will have a comfortable and safe learning experience.

2. How we will we put your money to use. We will do our research on schools that are not being treated fairly because of where their location is, and send the money you donated to their school board so they can upgrade their programs.
